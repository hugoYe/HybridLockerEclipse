Round and round it goes in this fast paced arcade game and where it stops nobody knows. In Circle Circus your avatar is rotating in different sized circles and all the control you have is a well timed click that will make him jump from the inside of the circle to the outside or vise versa. This way you can not only jump to another circle but also pick up treasures. On your way through the circles you can grab some valuable coins or sometimes even a bonus life. The coins can later be used to unlock new game characters and even to continue a game that is already lost. Play Circle Circus now for free and enjoy fast paced action and never ending challenges!
