Welcome to Minion Lab, the ultimate brain teasing game! In this fun online game it is your objective to place the sweet minions on the buttons that matches their color. Once you pushed a minion in a direction, it will only stop if it dashes into something. So you have to use obstacles or other minions to block their way and get them in the right places. You need to use as few moves, as possible to earn an award. Are you able to find the optimal solution for all the puzzles and earn three stars in each and every level? Now go to the Minion Lab, play this exciting game and help them to find their way!
