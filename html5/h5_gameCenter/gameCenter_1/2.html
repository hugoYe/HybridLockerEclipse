It is Valentine's Day, the best time of the year to delight your sweetheart with love and candies! With Candy Love Match you are now able to add all the fun and enjoyment of a classic match-3 puzzle game to the mix! You combine three or more items of the same type to make them vanish. If you manage to combine even more items, you get extras that are able to clear a complete row or column. But beware: From time to time a bomb will appear and you have only a few moves to defuse it. If you fail the bomb will blow away all the candy, your love you will lose everything. Play smart, make good use of the mighty boosters and have yourself a great Valentine's Day!
