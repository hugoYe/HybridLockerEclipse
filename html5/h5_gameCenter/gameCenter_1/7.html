Sometimes it is necessary to make a clear cut. While this is true for many things in life it is especially important if you have to share a cookie. Cute Cookie Cut is a unique puzzle game in which you have to slice a cookie into parts. While it is quite easy to just part the cookie it gets much harder, when you have to make sure that all parts are the same size. The more even your parts are, the more stars you awarded. Do you think you can get a perfect score for every level by collecting all the stars? Play Cute Cookie Cut now for free and find out! Get ready for some slicing fun!
