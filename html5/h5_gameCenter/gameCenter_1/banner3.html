You are on your own at a crossroad and you are surrounded by fierce ninjas. The ninjas will come after you from all directions and you need perfect timing to fight them off. Once they are right next to you, there is a small moment in time to kick in the direction of an attacker to neutralise him, before he is able to stab you. From time to time you fall in a frenzy that clears off all enemies at once and gives you some badly needed time to tense up and concentrate. The more enemies you managed to defeat, the cooler character you are able to unlock and play with. Be it a monk, the schoolgirl, a panda or even a robot - whatever you play, fun is guaranteed in this free online game. Play now!
